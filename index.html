About Us

Software Data Protection (SDP) is a code protection and enforcement platform that helps developers secure their software, websites, and applications against reverse engineering, unauthorized use, and misuse. We handle digital abuse reports on your behalf — so you don't have to deal with agencies like the FBI's cybercrime division yourself. Let us take that weight off your shoulders.

How It Works

SDP reviews, verifies, and submits reports of intellectual property misuse or unauthorized code use. Our team handles digital complaints and enforcement steps so developers can focus on building, not battling violations. We’re the buffer between you and complicated cybercrime processes.
